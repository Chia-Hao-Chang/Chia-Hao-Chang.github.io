Short Bio

Chia-Hao Chang is a first-year graduate student in the Decision, Information, and Communication Engineering (DICE) track of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) Department at the University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin). He received his Bachelor of Science (B.S.) degree in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Physics from National Taiwan University (NTU), Taipei, Taiwan in 2018. In NTU, he is a 3-time presidential award recipient.
