Short Bio

Chia-Hao Chang is a second-year graduate student in the Decision, Information, and Communication Engineering (DICE) track of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) Department at the University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin). He received his Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Physics from National Taiwan University (NTU), Taipei, Taiwan in 2018. In NTU, he was a 3-time presidential award recipient.

He has been working with Professor John Hasenbein since January 2019. Currently, he is working on modeling organ transplant systems with queueing models. He is also trying to realize how patients’ strategic behaviors may influence the overall transplant system performance.

Since June 2019 he has also been collaborating with Professor Thomas Wiseman. His research now focuses on learning, strategic experimentation and their applications.
